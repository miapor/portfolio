Navigating the World of Interactive Media Design:
A Student’s Perspective

Hey there! I’m Kimia Bagherpour, a 20-year-old student diving headfirst into the captivating world of Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. As I sip my coffee and gather my thoughts, I can’t help but reflect on the exciting journey that brought me here and the endless possibilities that lie ahead.
From a young age, I’ve always been drawn to creativity and technology. Whether it was sketching designs in my notebook or tinkering with website layouts, I knew that my passion lay at the intersection of art and digital innovation. That’s why when it came time to choose a path for my future, studying Interactive Media Design felt like the natural choice.
As I look ahead to the future, I’m filled with a sense of excitement and possibility. Whether it’s landing my dream job at a cutting-edge design firm or starting my own freelance business, I know that the skills and experiences I’ve gained at Algonquin College will serve me well in whatever path I choose to pursue.
